Equity premium puzzle

Peso Problem

Potential explanation to equity premium puzzle is as follows:

Investors put positive probability on catastrophic events that lower risk premia. This lower risk premia lowers consumption. But, we just never see these things in our data sample.

This is a specific example of a «rare events» literature. The main idea is that we are trying to guess at what investors might be observing, without being able to identify anything in our finite sample.

High risk aversion

Why not resolve EPP with high $\gamma$? It causes other problems, among which are:

Alternative preferences
